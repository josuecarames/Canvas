GINGER EPPINETTE

Director of Teaching & Learning

Learning and Development Specialist 

gingereppinette@fuller.edu

Ginger's background and training are as an educator and instructional designer. She taught in an inner-city school for six years before earning a master’s degree in Educational Technology and moving into district-level instructional technology and professional development roles for the ten years prior to her move to Fuller. She has written and administered several federal and state grants that provided a variety of technologies for classrooms and has designed, organized, and implemented professional development classes and programs in New Orleans, Baton Rouge, and Chicago.  In addition to directing the T&L office and team, Ginger designed and now manages the Instructional Improvement Mini-Grant Program for Fuller’s online instructors. She also leads the Women’s Leadership Faculty Cohort, which provides a community for online instructors, many of whom are not on the Pasadena campus. Ginger has a passion for the importance of building relationships and community in all areas of life but especially in online education. She believes the quality of relationships can be equal to and sometimes even better in online courses. If you’re interested in any of these things, let her know!

SARA WELLS

Curriculum Specialist & Instructional Designer
